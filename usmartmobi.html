usmartmobi

An e-commerce platform that enables people to buy certified devices and trade in their own mobile phones at the best price.


Product

usmartmobi is an e-commerce platform for buying and selling new and used tech products. It's built to provide end users the best and eco-friendly choice.

The complete vision for the platform is made up of two major aspects – e-commerce and ecosystem. The e-commerce section offers users to buy new, refurbished, and certified used techs. In addition, the trade-in feature is to encourage users to recycle their own devices during the check-out process. The ecosystem section provides users tech-related information to understand which product or service is best for you. Media room and repair info are the first two features launched at the initial stage.


My Role

As Product Manager, I worked closely with the VP, the EC account manager, and cross-functional teams from conception to launch. I drove the execution and success of the launch including defining scopes, planning, UX/UI design (user research, user flow diagrams, and wireframes), project management, and conducting user acceptance testing (UAT).
