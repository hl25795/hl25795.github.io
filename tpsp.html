TPSP

A Texas Foxconn internal ePortal for sharing the latest information of COVID-19 prevention and safety.


Product

TPSP, Texas Prevention and Safety ePortal, is a Foxconn internal website for sharing the latest information of COVID-19 prevention and safety with sites across Texas. The website has four features:
  1. Bulletin - a bulletin board for the latest COVID-19 information and local news
  2. Audit - a self audit system to report COVID-19 prevention checklists weekly for each site/zone
  3. EHS Community - a library to share EHS-related documents
  4. Situation Room - an online conference room for internal commanders to communicate confidential topics


My Role

I was a product manager assigned to create and launch the website in 2 weeks due to COVID-19 surge. In addition, I had additional responsibilities including UI design and front-end development.


Key Takeaways

  • Applied Agile software development method to release each feature every 2-3 days
  • Further developed my knowledge of SQL and corresponded to the questionnaire design
  • Implemented Bootstrap framework to create MVP and iterations
  • Collaborated with 10+ sites across Texas to extract relevant information and documents for sharing
