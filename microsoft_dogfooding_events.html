Microsoft Dogfooding Events

Microsoft Dogfooding Events of Windows 8 and Office 2013 had been held since August 2012 by Microsoft Taiwan.

Product

  • Windows 8, succeeding Windows 7, was introduced major changes to the platform and user interface including a new start screen, Microsoft app ecosystem, and touch-friendly design.
  • Office 2013, the successor to Microsoft Office 2010, supports online services and multi-touch interfaces.


My Role

I worked in the IT department to assist engineers and managers in:

  • Learning and testing new products to be launched; translating IT documents from English to Mandarin
  • Conducting a series of internal workshops and campaigns to encourage employees to update to the latest OS system/programs and utilize the new products to enhance their work efficiency
  • Collecting feedback via surveys after each events to improve user satisfaction
  • Designing monthly newsletters to share quick start guides for various Microsoft product lines and IT-related events
  • Troubleshooting and reporting IT issues to higher levels and other greater china branches
  • Organizing cross-functional department meetings and updating team statuses


Metrics

  • Learned Microsoft Access to create and maintain databases for internal asset management and system version control for each devices; rolled out Windows 8 upgrade to 500+ computer units during the dogfooding events
  • Hosted 30+ workshops and 300+ attendees in a half year with an 8.8 out of 10 average customer satisfaction rating
  • Achieved 93% FAE installment rate for Windows 8; outdid counterparts in other sub regions - the highest rate in the Greater China Region
