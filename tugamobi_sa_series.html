tugamobi SA Series

Brand

tugamobi is an American brand that specializes in providing the latest wearable and fitness devices. The flagship product lines include Stereo Audio (SA) series and Smart Band (SB) series, designed to cater to the needs of fitness enthusiasts and individuals seeking to track and monitor their health and wellness.


Product

The wearables market has experienced a steady rise in demand, fueled by the popularity of the fitness trend. With a range of products available, including smartwatches, fitness trackers, earbuds, wellness devices, and more, tugamobi recognized an opportunity to capitalize on this growing market by expanding its product line with a new range of fitness-related accessories.

The SA series was born after extensive market research and feedback collection from target audiences, with the SA01 launched in 2020 and the SA02 following the next year.


My Role

I played a role as a product manager and collaborated closely with tugamobi team consisted of a VP, an account manager, and 4 e-commerce team members.
  • Conducted market research to define product roadmap and specifications.
  • Developed a go-to-market strategy.
  • Designed product packages and campaign materials.
  • Communicated with electronics vendors from abroad to ensure timely shipment of products to USA.


Results

  • Successfully shipped products from concept to launch.
  • Optimized fulfillment process time by improving package design and relevant labels.
  • Analyzed the design of potential competitors and enhanced the aesthetics of package design and marketing campaigns.
  • Implemented supply chain management (SCM) and fulfillment operation KPIs such as Days of Supply (DOS) and On-time Shipping Indicator to improve efficiency and productivity.
