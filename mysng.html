MySNG

Company

LEADERG is an AI robotics company that specializes in the development of AI software, computers, and robots. The company has developed its own product lines since 2014.

Products

MySNG is LEADERG's first B2C product - a live streaming platform that's available on Windows, iOS, and Android. The platform enables users to broadcast live videos to friends and the public. In collaboration with animal shelters and veterinary hospitals, LEADERG launched a campaign to promote the awareness and adoption of stray animals using MySNG's video streaming service. This service allows for the instant display of interactions between stray animals and shelter volunteers, increasing the animals' chances of finding a forever home.

My Role

As a project planner, my role was to support product launch events and product improvement. I was responsible for planning launch campaigns, testing product prototypes, and creating product instructions and requirements documentation.
  • Collaborated with various stakeholders, including the CEO, development teams, and external clients to ensure that product launches were successful and met customer needs.
  • Gathered feedback from users and clients to improve the product. This involved conducting surveys, focus groups, and user testing to understand how customers were using the product and what improvements could be made.
  • Tested product prototypes to ensure that they were functional and met the desired specifications.
  • Created product documentation, including instructions and requirements, to ensure that customers could easily understand and use the product.
