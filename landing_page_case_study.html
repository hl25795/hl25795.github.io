Landing Page Case Study

The aim of the project is to design a landing page for mock technical interviews and develop a prototype for testing user preferences of the upcoming mock interview service. The case study and design consist of three activities:

  • Research existing products and potential user needs
  • Create a landing page wireframe for cross-functional team discussions
  • Determine metrics for product launch success
